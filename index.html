Frequently Asked Questions

At our coffee shop, we prioritize your satisfaction and strive to make every cup a memorable experience.

What types of coffee do you offer?

We offer a variety of coffee blends, including Arabica, Robusta, and specialty blends sourced from different regions around the world.

Do you offer decaffeinated coffee?

Yes, we offer decaf options for those who prefer to enjoy coffee without the caffeine content.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and weekends from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer vegan options?

Yes, we offer a selection of vegan-friendly coffee drinks and treats to accommodate different dietary preferences.
